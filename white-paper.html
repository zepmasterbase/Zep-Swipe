Zep Cash

The Student Economy Starts Here

Zukma Network is a digital ecosystem powering Africa’s student and freelancer economy through real-world blockchain utility. At its core, Zep Cash enables fast, practical transactions across the network. In Zep Swipe, it powers freelancer payments for tasks and gigs. On Campus Wave, it is used for tipping and peer-to-peer rewards within student communities. Together, Zukma Network and Zep Cash turn everyday student activity into a connected, value-driven digital economy.
